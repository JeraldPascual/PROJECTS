MENTAL HEALTH AWARENESS

Our mental health is one the most important key feature in our daily life, therefore, we must do our best in order to keep it healthy just like our physical health.

This survey aims to:

  • Further advance our knowledge of the City College Students mental health.
  • Increase the awareness of the students of their own mental health.
  • It intends to serve as a guide for students to maintain healthy mental health.
